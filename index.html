FAQ

Everything
You Need to Know.

What is Future Fashion Festival? +
Future Fashion Festival by House of XIX is India's next-generation fashion and innovation festival — a two-day event bringing together designers, creators, brands, models, students, innovators, and industry leaders to discover, celebrate, and elevate the future of fashion.
Who can attend the festival? +
The festival is open to anyone passionate about fashion, creativity, and innovation. This includes fashion students, young professionals, industry veterans, brand representatives, press, investors, and general public tickets will be available closer to the event.
How do I apply for the competitions? +
Click the "Apply Now" button on any competition section and complete the online application form. You'll need to submit your portfolio, a short bio, and answer a few brief questions about your work and vision. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis.
Is there an age limit for competitions? +
Most competition categories are open to participants aged 16–30. The Innovation Challenge is open up to age 35. All participants must be at least 16 years of age at the time of application.
Do I need a formal degree to apply? +
No. We believe in talent over credentials. Whether you studied at a premier design institution or are completely self-taught, we evaluate all applications on the quality, originality, and potential of your work — not your certificate.
How do I apply as a runway model? +
Model applications are open via the Runway section of the website. Submit your comp card or portfolio, measurements, and a brief introduction. Shortlisted models will be contacted for an in-person or virtual casting call.
How do I apply to showcase my collection on the runway? +
Designer runway applications are separate from competition entries. Submit your portfolio, a collection concept, and 3–5 sample looks. A jury of industry professionals will review all applications and select designers based on design quality, concept strength, and readiness for professional presentation.
Are there tickets for the general public? +
Yes. General public tickets for the Future Talks, Innovation Hub, and Awards Gala will be available closer to the festival date. Join our mailing list to be the first to know when tickets go on sale and to access early-bird pricing.
How can my brand become a sponsor? +
Email our partnerships team at thenineteen.in@gmail.com to request the full sponsor deck and initiate a conversation. We offer custom partnership structures and are happy to design a collaboration that aligns with your brand's specific goals and audience.
Where will the festival be held? +
The festival venue will be announced shortly. The first edition will take place in a major Indian metropolitan city. Sign up to our mailing list for the venue reveal and to stay updated on all festival announcements.
Can I apply from outside India? +
While the festival is currently India-focused, we welcome applications from the Indian diaspora and international participants who are working on projects relevant to Indian or South Asian fashion. Final round participation requires physical presence at the festival.
Is there a registration fee for competitions? +
A nominal application fee applies to competition entries to ensure seriousness of participation. Details are available on the individual competition application pages. Shortlisted finalists may be eligible for travel and accommodation support — details announced per competition category.
How are competition winners selected? +
All competitions are judged by a panel of senior industry professionals across fashion, design, technology, and business. Judging criteria are specific to each category and are published on the application pages. The jury's decision is final, with the exception of the People's Choice Award, which is voted by the festival audience.
Can I apply to speak at Future Talks? +
Yes. Speaker applications are open for individuals who have a compelling story, original insight, or transformative idea at the intersection of fashion and one of our core themes. Submit a speaker proposal including your bio, proposed topic, and a brief talk outline via the contact page.
How do I stay updated on festival news? +
Join our mailing list, follow us on Instagram and LinkedIn, and subscribe to our YouTube channel for behind-the-scenes content, speaker announcements, competition updates, and ticket information. All major updates are sent to subscribers first.
